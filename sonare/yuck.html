HCI+D Project: Music Practicing Platform

Sonare's Development Journey.

Project Overview

Value Proposition: Enhance musical practice through community engagement and collaborative tools.

Problem Statement: Musicians often practice in isolation without easy access to collaborative tools and community feedback.

Solution Overview: A comprehensive platform that not only tracks individual progress but also allows for mixing tracks, sharing music sheets, and community engagement.

Design Process

Needfinding

Initial research phase involving interviews and analysis to understand musicians' experiences, challenges, and emotions when learning new songs.

View Needfinding Report

Points of View & HMWs

Developing Point of View (POV) statements based on needfinding insights and generating "How Might We" (HMW) questions to frame design challenges.
