What is Sonare?

Sonare is a comprehensive platform that not only tracks individual progress but also allows for mixing tracks, sharing music sheets, and community engagement.

Our project followed the human-centered design process, starting with needfinding to understand user needs, followed by ideation with "How Might We" questions, and then iterating through low, medium, and high-fidelity prototypes. Each stage built upon the insights from the previous one, leading to our final design solution.
